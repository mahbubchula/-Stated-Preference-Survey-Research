# Assignment 1 - Reflection on Stated Preference Opportunities

**Linked module:** [Module 1 - Foundations](../modules/01-foundations.md)

## Objective
Assess your current or aspirational transportation program and articulate where SP research would add decision value beyond existing RP datasets.

## Deliverables
1. **Context snapshot (300 words):** describe the transport issue, stakeholders, and existing data limitations.
2. **SP opportunity matrix:** table with at least three candidate SP research questions mapping to policy levers.
3. **Risk/benefit commentary (200 words):** discuss uncertainties, potential biases, and how SP could influence action.

## Format & submission
- Submit a 2-3 page PDF or Markdown document.
- Include citations for any studies or datasets referenced.
- Name the file `M1_reflection_<lastname>.pdf` (or `.md`).

## Evaluation rubric
| Criterion | Weight | Indicators |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Problem framing | 30% | Clear linkage to transport objectives, relevant stakeholders |
| SP justification | 40% | Demonstrates why SP is superior/complementary to RP data |
| Critical thinking | 20% | Identifies risks, limitations, and mitigation |
| Clarity & professionalism | 10% | Structure, tone, formatting |

## Suggested references
- Ben-Akiva & Lerman (1985) - Intro chapters.
- ITF/OECD SP primer (2020).
- Agency reports in your local context (attach links if public).
